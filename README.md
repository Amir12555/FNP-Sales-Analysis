# FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

**Organization:** Ferns N Petals (FNP)
**Project Type:** Sales & Business Performance Analysis

Ferns N Petals (FNP) is one of India’s leading gifting and flower delivery brands, operating in a highly competitive and time-sensitive market. This project focuses on analyzing FNP’s historical sales data to extract actionable insights that support strategic and operational decision-making.

The FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard provides a consolidated and interactive view of revenue performance, customer purchasing behavior, product category contribution, occasion-based demand, and regional sales trends. The primary goal of this project is to assist business stakeholders in identifying revenue drivers, optimizing marketing strategies, and improving operational efficiency through data-driven insights.

---

## Project Objectives

The key objectives of this project are:

* To analyze overall sales and revenue performance
* To identify high-revenue occasions and top-performing products
* To understand customer spending patterns and purchasing behavior
* To evaluate delivery efficiency and operational performance
* To analyze regional demand and city-wise order distribution

---

## Scope of Analysis

The analysis is based on historical sales data covering:

* Orders placed across multiple occasions and festivals
* A wide range of gifting product categories
* Multiple cities and regions across India
* Different time dimensions, including hourly, monthly, and seasonal trends

The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly interpret performance trends and make informed business decisions.

---

## Business Problem and Importance

FNP operates in an emotion-driven gifting market where customer expectations are high and timely delivery is critical. Without effective data analysis, it becomes challenging to:

* Identify peak demand periods accurately
* Plan inventory and supply chains efficiently
* Design targeted and personalized marketing campaigns
* Maintain consistent revenue throughout the year

This project addresses these challenges by converting raw sales data into meaningful business intelligence, enabling improved customer satisfaction, operational efficiency, and profitability.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights the following key performance metrics:

* **Total Orders:** 1,000
* **Total Revenue:** ₹35,20,984
* **Average Customer Spend:** ₹3,520.98
* **Average Order-to-Delivery Time:** 5.53

### KPI Interpretation

* The average customer spend reflects a strong preference for premium gifting products
* The delivery timeline indicates efficient logistics and fulfillment operations
* Overall KPIs demonstrate stable and healthy sales performance

---

## Occasion-Based Revenue Analysis

Occasion-wise analysis highlights the emotional nature of customer purchases:

* Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan generate the highest revenue
* Birthdays and Holi contribute consistent revenue throughout the year
* Valentine’s Day and Diwali contribute relatively lower revenue than expected

**Business Insight:**
Occasion-specific personalization, targeted promotions, and customized gift recommendations can significantly improve average order value during high-impact events.

---

## Product Category Performance

Analysis of product categories reveals clear customer preferences:

* Colours and Soft Toys are the top-performing categories
* Sweets and Plants maintain steady demand across multiple occasions
* Cakes and Mugs show moderate performance

**Business Insight:**
Bundled offerings and combo packages using high-performing categories can enhance cross-selling and increase overall revenue.

---

## Time-Based Ordering Behavior

Order timing analysis shows:

* Peak revenue generation during evening hours
* Reduced order activity during early morning hours

**Business Insight:**
Digital marketing efforts such as advertisements, email campaigns, and push notifications should be scheduled during peak evening hours to improve conversion rates.

---

## Monthly Revenue Trends and Seasonality

Monthly revenue analysis indicates strong seasonality:

* Revenue peaks during months with major festivals and celebrations
* Off-season months experience lower order volumes and revenue

**Business Insight:**
Off-season initiatives such as corporate gifting, subscription-based gifting, and curated self-care packages can help stabilize revenue throughout the year.

---

## Product and City-Wise Performance

### Top Revenue-Generating Products

* Magnum Set
* Quia Gift
* Harum Pack

These products demonstrate strong premium appeal and repeat purchase behavior.

### City-Wise Order Analysis

* Cities such as Imphal, Kavali, and Dhanbad record high order volumes
* Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities contribute significantly to overall revenue

**Business Insight:**
Localized marketing strategies and strengthened last-mile delivery networks can further improve regional performance.

---

## Operational Performance

* The average order-to-delivery time of 5.53 days reflects efficient order fulfillment
* Consistent delivery performance strengthens customer trust and repeat purchase behavior

---

## Conclusion and Business Impact

This Sales Analysis Dashboard enables FNP management to:

* Identify high-value products and key revenue-generating occasions
* Optimize marketing strategies and campaign timing
* Improve inventory planning and supply chain efficiency
* Expand and strengthen presence in high-growth regional markets

Overall, this project demonstrates how sales analytics and data visualization dashboards play a critical role in supporting business growth, enhancing customer experience, and improving revenue predictability in a competitive gifting market.
